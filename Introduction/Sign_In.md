## Login Dialog

When you run **Stimulsoft Server**, the first menu displayed is user authentication. Here you must specify the username and password of an account to get to the workspace. Below is the login dialog:


![](../images/log_in_window.png)


![](../images/img_1.png) In this field, you enter a user name (login) of your account. A login account can be an email address provided in the process of registration.

![](../images/img_2.png) In this field, you specify the current password for your account.

![](../images/img_3.png) If you check this flag, the Login and Password fields will be automatically populated with authentication information, using which the last entry was made with the computer.

![](../images/img_4.png) When you click this button, the entered authentication information is verified. If authentication credentials are correct, there will be signing in.

![](../images/img_5.png) Click this item, if you need to restore your password.

![](../images/img_6.png) This button redirects you to the registration form of a new account with a new workspace.

**Sign Up**

If you want to register a new account and workspace, go to the Registration menu and fill in the user profile.


![](../images/sing_up_window.png)


![](../images/img_1.png) Put your first name.

![](../images/img_2.png) Put your second name.

![](../images/img_3.png) Type your email address (login) of the future profile. You should remember that the email address provided here is the login to access the account.

![](../images/img_4.png) Type the password to your account. Minimum 6 characters are allowed in the password.

![](../images/img_5.png) Go to the terms of the license agreement. Do not forget to read this.

![](../images/img_6.png) After clicking this button, a new account with a working space will be created.

![](../images/img_7.png) Click this menu item if you do not need registration or already have the account.

**Forgot Password**

Sometimes you need to log in to your account but forgot your password. To recover your password, use the following menu.

![](../images/reset_password_window.png)


![](../images/img_1.png) This field specifies the Username (email address) that is used for logging into your account.

![](../images/img_2.png) After you click this button, your current password will be reset, and a new one will be created and sent to the email address specified when filling the registration form.

![](../images/img_3.png) This button redirects you to the registration form of a new account with a new workspace.

![](../images/img_4.png) This button closes the menu.
