## Tab Preview


With the tab **Preview**, you can preview the report before printing, exporting, sending, or any other action. You can also see a preview of the report can be in a separate window of the viewer.


![](../../../images/topics/Report_Designer.Tabs.Tab_Preview_1.png)


![](../../../images/img_1.png) Runs a report for printing.

![](../../../images/img_2.png) [Saves](../../../Viewer/Exports/index.md) the report.

![](../../../images/img_3.png) Shows or hides the parameters panel in the report.

![](../../../images/img_4.png) Shows or hides the bookmarks tree. The viewer automatically identifies whether there are bookmarks in the report.

![](../../../images/img_5.png) Enables the Search bar.
